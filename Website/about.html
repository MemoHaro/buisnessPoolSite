About Us

Guillermo's Pool Services, Inc. was founded by Guillermo Haro in 2020 and is based in the San Fernando Valley, Serving the Los Angeles, Ventura, Santa Barbara, and Orange Counties. We are an authorized applicator for Pebble Tec Superior Quality pool finishes as well as offering an array of different pool finishes to suit your personal needs. In additional to our pool finishes we also offer exceptional customer service.

We Provide Services to the Following Cities, Towns and Surrounding Regions of CA:

  • Sylmar
  • Los Angeles
  • Ventura
  • Santa Barbara
  • San Fernando
  • Burbank
  • Santa Clarita
  • Santa Monica
  • Pasadena
  • Orange County

What We Do

We specialize in pool plastering but can provide a wide variety of different services. We provide exceptional customer service.
